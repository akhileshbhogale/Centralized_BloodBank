We are a platform that Bloodbanks,Hospitals as well as individual users can use to donate as well update their blood units. We aim to bring them together on a common platform.A+,A-,B+,B-,AB+,AB-,O+,O- are the various blood groups and their corresponding blood bag types that are:-Single(contains CPDA-1 anticoagulant solution),Double(Double Blood Bag is designed for the collection and separation of whole blood into two different blood components, plasma and red cells),Triple(used to collect and separate three different blood components: plasma, red cells and buffy coat).
